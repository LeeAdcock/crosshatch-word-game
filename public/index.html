Crosshatch

The daily uncrossword game

How to play

Crosshatch is a daily uncrossword: you're handed the words and an empty, endless board — build the tightest crossword you can. You'll place 25 words in all, and the game is over once the last one is down. Everyone gets the same board each day.

Playing

  • Drag a word from the Word Bank onto the board. It auto-rotates between across and down to find a legal fit — there's no rotate button.
  • Drag empty space to pan the infinite board.
  • A green highlight means the word fits and can be dropped there. If it doesn't turn green, the placement isn't legal.

The rules

  • Every new word must connect to the board — cross an existing word or sit right beside one.
  • Overlapping letters must match.
  • Every run of two or more letters created — across and down — must be a real word.

Scoring

  • You're rewarded for packing tightly: score grows with the value of your letters per square of board used, multiplied by how many words you've placed. Compact, high-value boards win.
  • Forming several words in one drop earns combo bonuses (Double word!, Triple word!, and up).
  • Every few placements you earn a 🎁 bonus word (an amber tile pinned to the top of the bank). It's chosen just for you — a word that slots perfectly into your crossword and no one else's. Play it next for big points; it expires if you place anything else first.
